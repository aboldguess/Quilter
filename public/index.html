Patchwork Tile Helper

Select the current payday, then add tiles. Use the sliders to set buttons (0–3), cost (0–10) and movement (0–6). Use the yellow or green "Buy" buttons to record who purchased a tile. Tap cells to draw shapes and choose a color. Use "Export Library" to download your pieces and "Import Library" to load a saved file. Use "New Game" to reset purchases. Tap column headers to sort pieces.
